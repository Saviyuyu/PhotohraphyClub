About Us

At Photography Club, we share a passion for photography and the art of capturing the world around us. Through regular training sessions and outings that explore various aspects of photo-taking, we strive to sharpen our skills and create wonderful stories through our lenses. As the eyes of our school, we document events, performances, competitions, and more across campus, preserving precious memories for years to come. Some of our best shots are even featured on this website!.
